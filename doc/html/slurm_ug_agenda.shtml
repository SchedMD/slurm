Slurm User Group Meeting 2020

The Slurm User Group Meeting 2020 will be held on 15 and 16 September 2020 in Cambridge, Massachusetts, at the Richard A. and Susan F. Smith Campus Center and is hosted by Harvard University and SchedMD.

Registration

The meeting cost is:

  • $325 per person for early registration by 1 July 2020
  • $400 per person for standard registration by 15 August 2020
  • $700 per person for late registration by 31 August 2020

This includes presentations, tutorials, lunch and snacks on both days, plus dinner on Tuesday evening.

This year, there is an additional option of attending a one-day Slurm Boot Camp on 14 September. This boot camp is designed for system administrators who are relatively new to Slurm or those who want to review the basics of Slurm, and include registration for the Slurm User Group Meeting as well.
