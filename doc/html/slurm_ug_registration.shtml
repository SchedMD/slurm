Meeting Information

The meeting will be held at California State University's Conference Center, 1000 Broadway Avenue, Suite 109, Oakland, California (Phone 510-208-7001, access from 11th Street). This state of the art facility is located adjacent to the 12th Street BART (Metro) station, with easy access to the entire San Fransisco area. There is also frequent and free bus service to Jack London Square using the Broadway Shuttle.

Hotel Information

Many hotel options are available in Oakland, San Fransisco, and elsewhere in the area. Consider the hotels listed below as suggestions:

Oakland Marriott City Center
Across the street from the conference center and BART. Rooms are available to government employees at a rate of $140 per night. Rooms are available to others at a rate of $209 per night. A block of rooms have been reserved for the conference at this rate. When making reservations, please say that you are attending the Slurm User Group Meeting to take advantage of these rates.

Waterfront Hotel
Like it says in the name, on the waterfront, with several nice restaurants nearby. About 1 mile (2 km) from the conference center via the Broadway Shuttle. Ferry service to San Fransisco adjacent to the hotel.
