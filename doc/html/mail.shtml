Mailing Lists

We maintain two Slurm mailing lists:

  • slurm-announce@schedmd.com is designated for communications about SLURM releases [low traffic].
  • slurm-dev@schedmd.com is designated for communications to SLURM developers [high traffic at times].

To subscribe to either list, vist the link below, sign up and get involved!

http://lists.schedmd.com/cgi-bin/dada/mail.cgi/list

You can likewise unsubscribe from either list at the same link.

The archive of the slurm-dev list is online:
http://dir.gmane.org/gmane.comp.distributed.slurm.devel
and
http://groups.google.com/group/slurm-devel
