Directions to Slurm User Group 2010

Slurm User Group 2010 will take place at hotel and conference center Novotel Paris Gare de Lyon in Paris downtown. Informations about location and access to the conference center can be found here.

The conference center is located near the regional train line RER A station "Gare de Lyon".
Informations about the regional train and metro company RATP can be found here.

Directions from Paris Airports

Two international airports are located at North-Est and South of Paris, Paris-Charles de Gaulle and Paris-Orly. Directions from these airports to Paris downtown can be found from Paris Airports website.

The regional train line RER B is the easiest way to reach Paris downtown from these locations. RER B and RER A are linked at the station "Chatelet - Les Halles".
RER A station "Gare de Lyon" is the first one after this point in direction of "Marne-la-vallée - Chessy" or "Boissy saint-léger".

Directions from Paris National Train Stations

Paris national train stations are all linked to the RATP RER and Metro system. You can get informations on how to reach RER A station "Gare de Lyon" from RATP website.
